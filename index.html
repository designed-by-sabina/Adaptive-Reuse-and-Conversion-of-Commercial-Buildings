1. 10_Visual Representation_
I want to use architectural linework as inspiration for my minimalist aesthetic. I envision a white background and a visual vocabulary consisting of various line types and abstract representations of architectural material textures (concrete, brick, etc.). I want the composition to feel sharp yet playful, with multiple parti diagrams layered on top of one another. I would define my visual language as a minimalist architectural graphic language that combines precise linework, layered diagrammatic drawings, and abstract representations of material textures.

11_Rhetorical Argument_
The complexity of adaptive reuse projects is what makes them less attractive to construction industry professionals and investors. The historic preservation component can be particularly challenging to execute, and many clients would rather demolish historic facades and other architectural elements (or even the entire building) than preserve them. Today, driven by the housing crisis, the conversion of commercial buildings into residential use is becoming increasingly common. Digital technologies such as 3D scanning, the creation of digital twins, and AI-powered "seeing through walls" tools, such as those being developed by Arcadis and Autodesk, have the potential to simplify one of the most challenging aspects of adaptive reuse: coordinating existing conditions with the proposed design.

12_Outline of a potential capstone project_
A computational design tool for identifying structural and other building elements from point clouds and generating BIM-compatible digital twins. My primary interests lie in structural and facade design, particularly in the challenges of coordinating newly designed elements with existing building conditions.

13_The Challenge_
I anticipate that the primary challenge of this project will be my limited experience with coding and machine learning. Acquiring building point clouds will require access to building sites, while training the machine learning model will require a dataset of labeled building elements.

An alternative direction for the project would be the development of a computational design tool that proposes modifications to commercial buildings based on the distance between the building core and the exterior walls. In this case, I would need to create a dataset of commercial buildings, including the location and dimensions of their cores, to support the development of the tool.

Proposal for Drawing Type_
A map visualizing vacant commercial real estate across New York City, highlighting areas with the greatest potential for adaptive reuse and commercial-to-residential conversion.

Proposal for Material Gesture_
An interactive installation consisting of a series of scaled plaster building cores representing different commercial building typologies. An overhead projector would overlay the existing building footprints and demonstrate how floor plates are modified during the conversion from commercial to residential use.
